Who We Are

Go To Market Solutions Limited (GTM) is an East Africa–based FMCG distribution and brand-building company dedicated to connecting quality brands with consumers across the region. We specialize in foods, personal care, and home care products that enhance everyday living. With an experienced management team and strong regional trade networks, GTM serves as a strategic bridge between brands and East African households, ensuring efficient market access, visibility, and growth.

Vision

To be the partner of choice for suppliers of quality foods, personal care, and home products in East Africa.

Mission

Deliver trusted, high‑quality FMCG brands through efficient distribution, innovative marketing, and long‑term partnerships.
